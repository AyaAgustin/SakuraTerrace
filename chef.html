HISTORY

SAKURA TERRACE




GOOD FOOD GOOD LIFE

In 1985, when the concept of “fast food” was rapidly entering the country, Sakura Terrace saw the opportunity of bringing Japanese cuisine to the Philippines.

The first Sakura Terrace restaurant was opened in Military Cut-off, Located in Baguio City,Philippines. Sakura Terrace built an incredibly dedicated following with its combination of quality, value, and abundance.

While other Japanese restaurants at the time focused on delivering more traditional experiences, Sakura Terrace strove to give its customers a modern, mainstream approach to Japanese cuisine.

With innovation as one of its Food Promise Pillars, Sakura Terrace is known for its signature Japanese dishes such as Japanese Rice, Ramen, Salmon Punch Rolls, Shrimp Tepura Rolls, and many more Japanese variety delicious foods.

Today, Sakura Terrace is an established name in Filipino dining, to let people experience Japanese food culture in a near and convinient place.

Our Mission

Make everyone experience a taste of Japan.

At Tokyo Tokyo, we continuously strive to provide customers with a variety of high-quality Japanese dishes. Over the years, we have become the country’s #1 Japanese food chain bringing Japan closer to Filipinos.

We aim to make Japanese food accessible to everyone.We are committed to delivering constant improvement in our products and service to give our customers a satisfying and memorable dining experience.

Our Vision

Our customers should experience the culture, and the dishes which are the same as those found if they actually traveled to Japan for their meal.
